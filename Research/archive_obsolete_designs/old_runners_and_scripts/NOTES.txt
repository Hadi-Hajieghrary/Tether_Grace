Old runners and post-processing scripts
=======================================
Purpose: Orchestration / analysis scripts from earlier phases.

Files:
- phase2_campaign_runner.py          Phase-2 campaign orchestrator.
- run_full_campaign.sh               Early 6-scenario shell runner
                                     (soft-rope era, before IEEE figure style).
- run_four_drones_scenarios.sh       Box-only 4-drone runner.
- four_drones_meshcat_replay.py      pydrake-based replay attempt — replaced
                                     once Meshcat recording was moved into
                                     the C++ sim itself.
- scripts/                           Phase-1 analyze_* Python tools for
                                     the early single-drone MPC work.

Why kept: reference only. They illustrate how the campaign was run in
earlier phases. They generally target old source files that are now in
sibling archive folders here — so re-running them would need the
corresponding old targets restored in CMakeLists.

Active counterpart: Research/run_ieee_campaign.sh  +  Research/analysis/ieee/
