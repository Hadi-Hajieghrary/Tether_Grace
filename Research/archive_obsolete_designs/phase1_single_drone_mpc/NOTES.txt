Phase 1 — single-drone decentralized MPC
=========================================
Era: early Phase 1 of the research programme.

Purpose: First attempt at an explicit model-predictive controller using
SNOPT over a short horizon, applied to one quadrotor carrying a payload
via a single rope. Meant to serve as the N = 1 base case for later
extension to multi-drone.

Files:
- decentralized_mpc_test_main.cc   Top-level sim harness.
- decentralized_optimal_controller.{cc,h}
                                   The SNOPT MPC controller (abandoned).
- plant_state_splitter.{cc,h}      Utility that sliced the plant state
                                   vector into drone-position, rope-tension,
                                   and cable-direction signals.

Why superseded: the MPC never tracked reference properly (trajectory-tracking
cost had no gradient in decision variables — see discussion in the
session memory); replaced by a simple cascade PD, which later evolved into
the peer-aware controller and finally into the fully-local QP controller.

Replaced by: Research/cpp/src/decentralized_local_controller.cc
