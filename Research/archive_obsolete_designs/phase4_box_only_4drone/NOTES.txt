Early Phase 4 — four-drone box-only test
========================================
Era: first attempt at a four-drone fleet, before switching to URDF quadrotors.

Purpose: Extend the Phase-2.5 architecture to N = 4 drones. Used simple
coloured box visuals (no URDF model) to keep the plant tractable while
the team nailed down the load-sharing dynamics.

Files:
- decentralized_mpc_four_drones_test_main.cc  Top-level 4-drone sim harness.
- quad_plant_state_splitter.{cc,h}            Four-drone version of the
                                              plant-state splitter.

Why superseded: early validation only; payload-tracking was jumpy and the
box visuals were unsuitable for paper-quality replay. Replaced in quick
succession by: (a) a proper URDF-based 4-drone sim with the peer-aware
controller, and (b) the fully-local QP controller.

Replaced by: Research/cpp/src/decentralized_local_controller.cc
            Research/cpp/src/decentralized_fault_aware_sim_main.cc (URDF)
