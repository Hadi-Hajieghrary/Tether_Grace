Phase 2.5 — inter-drone tension communication
=============================================
Era: Phase 2.5 of the research programme.

Purpose: Added an explicit inter-drone communication channel: each drone
publishes its local rope tension, and all peers subscribe to the aggregated
N-vector. A multi-rope ForceCombiner merges the per-drone rope forces into
the plant's single applied-spatial-force input port.

Files:
- decentralized_mpc_phase25_test_main.cc  Top-level 2-drone sim harness
                                          with comms enabled.
- force_combiner.{cc,h}                   Multiplexer that sums 4+ rope
                                          force-vector streams into one
                                          plant input.
- tension_communicator.{cc,h}             Publishes/subscribes scalar
                                          tension over the (simulated)
                                          shared bus.

Why superseded: the explicit tension broadcast and N_alive estimator gave
a clean fault-tolerance story but is strictly more complex than necessary.
The fully-local QP controller uses only the drone's OWN rope tension and
the shared payload-reference trajectory — load rebalancing after a cable
severance is emergent from the coupled rope-payload physics, not from any
peer communication.

Replaced by: Research/cpp/src/decentralized_local_controller.cc
            (no peer-tension input port at all — truly fully local)
