Baseline simulations kept from earlier phases
=============================================
Purpose: Reference / regression harnesses from Tether_Lift baselines.
These were useful in earlier phases for benchmarking the new decentralized
controller against the unmodified single-loop GPAC lift controller from
Tether_Lift (and for smoke-testing the multibody plant assembly before
our own controller was ready).

Files:
- fault_injection_main.cc   Wraps Tether_Lift's unmodified controllers in
                            our sim harness with a CableFaultGate — used
                            as a Phase-1/2 regression reference.
- gpac_fault_sim_main.cc    GPAC-specific variant (adds an extended-state
                            observer plus concurrent-learning adaptive
                            load estimator around the same Tether_Lift
                            inner-loop controllers).

Why kept: both are fully-wired working sims, independently useful for
"baseline vs our-controller" comparisons in future paper revisions. They
are NOT compiled by the trimmed CMakeLists; if you want to revive them,
restore their `add_executable` blocks (the source files are right here).

Active counterpart: Research/cpp/src/decentralized_fault_aware_sim_main.cc
