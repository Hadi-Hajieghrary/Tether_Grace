# Archive — prior campaign outputs

These are the raw outputs of earlier sim campaigns that preceded the
current IEEE campaign. Kept for provenance; nothing here is part of the
current paper artefacts (those are in the sibling `00_system_architecture/`
… `08_source_data/` folders).

| Folder | Era | Contents | Size |
|--------|-----|----------|------|
| `decentralized_replays/` | pre-IEEE-style campaigns (v1–v6) | per-scenario `.html` + `.csv` + `.png` bundles produced by `run_full_campaign.sh` during the peer-aware-controller era | ≈ 496 MB |
| `four_drones_replays/` | early box-only 4-drone tests | Meshcat HTML replays from `run_four_drones_scenarios.sh`, when quadrotor visuals were plain coloured boxes (not yet URDF meshes) | ≈ 13 MB |
| `campaign_execution.log` | Phase-2 runner log | Text log from the Phase-2 campaign orchestrator | 4 KB |

The runner scripts that generated these outputs are archived at
`Research/archive_obsolete_designs/old_runners_and_scripts/`.
